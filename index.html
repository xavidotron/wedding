Sarah and Xavid's Wicked Quirky Wedding

The wedding celebration of Xavid Pretzer and Sarah Grace Tuttle will be held on June 23rd, 2018 at 2pm at Danehy Park, Cambridge, Massachusetts.

Reception with dinner to follow at the First Church in Belmont Unitarian Universalist.

Please note, we have elected to have a dry wedding. Instead, we'll have other fun things! Thank you for respecting our wishes.

Weather

There is no tent allowed at Danehy Park. If we need to change locations due to weather, the ceremony will take place at The First Church in Belmont reception site instead. If we are changing ceremony locations, we will update this website by 1 PM to let you know!

Directions and Parking

The ceremony is at Danehy Park in Cambridge. Park off New Street (an approximate address is 166 New St, Cambridge, MA 02140), near the dog park. Allow time for a walk from the parking lot to the ceremony location, as shown on this map.

The reception will be at The First Church in Belmont Unitarian Universalist at 404 Concord Ave, Belmont, MA 02478. Parking is available there, with overflow on-street parking and if necessary the nearby municipal lot at 10 Claflin St, Belmont, MA 02478.

Accessibility

The paved path to the ceremony includes some gentle inclines, declines, and a ramp. The cermony itself is on grass that may have some small bumps.

The reception venue is ADA accessible and has an elevator.
